A Cool Piano Playing MIDI Effect

by AJ Pisani


How To Use:

First,

  1. Select Input and Output
  2. Choose a Speed
  3. Pick a Chord Quality
Speed Setting: 2 (Default)

Now, Eveything Under Middle C will Play a pattern with the bass notes
Just play the root of the chord in bass like youre playing whole notes
Then play your melody above middle C!
